There is one thing everyone has in common: amazing life stories to tell. What if you could rebuild these stories, find and connect with old friends, learn other interesting stories around your own, and have a fun and bonding moment with your family and friends in the process? That's what Reminiscens is about. A tablet application to share, explore and rediscover our life stories in a novel and fun way. Take a look to our demonstration video to see the application in action and let your imagination fly with it!
